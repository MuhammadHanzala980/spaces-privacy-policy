What gets deleted

  • Your profile — name, email, avatar
  • All posts and comments you created

  • All uploaded media (photos, files)

⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be removed within 30 days of your request.

How to request deletion

1
Send an email to our support address below with the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
2
Include the email address associated with your Spaces account so we can identify it.
3
We will confirm your request within 3 business days and permanently delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

Retention policy

After receiving your deletion request, we will permanently delete all your personal data within 30 days. Some anonymized activity data (e.g. aggregate post counts) may be retained for platform analytics but will not be linked to your identity.
