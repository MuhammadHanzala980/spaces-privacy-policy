Child Safety Standards

Spaces  ·  Effective date: April 21, 2026

Our Commitment

Spaces is committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users and has a zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) in any form. We strictly prohibit any content, behavior, or activity that endangers minors.

Prohibited Content & Behavior

The following are strictly prohibited on Spaces:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) of any kind
  • Sexual content involving minors
  • Grooming, solicitation, or exploitation of minors
  • Any content that sexualizes or endangers children
  • Sharing, distributing, or requesting any material that exploits minors

App Access & Age Restriction

Spaces is intended for users aged 18 and above. Users must be 18 or older to register and use the platform. We do not knowingly allow minors to create accounts on Spaces.

Reporting Child Safety Concerns

If you encounter any content or behavior on Spaces that you believe violates child safety standards or involves the exploitation or abuse of a minor, please report it immediately:

  • Email us at tobiaszhuang@gmail.com with the subject line "Child Safety Report"
  • Include as much detail as possible — screenshots, user IDs, or content descriptions
  • All reports are treated with strict confidentiality and reviewed promptly

Enforcement & Reporting to Authorities

Spaces takes all child safety reports seriously. Upon identifying any CSAM or child exploitation content:

  • The content and associated account will be immediately removed
  • The incident will be reported to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) as required by law
  • Where applicable, we will cooperate fully with local and national law enforcement authorities
